About Our Data

Our comprehensive healthcare data sets combine information from the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) with proprietary demand potential analytics. CMS is the federal agency that administers Medicare, Medicaid, and other health insurance programs, maintaining extensive databases of healthcare providers and facilities nationwide.

This combined data allows you to identify and analyze healthcare providers within a specified radius of any potential address. By integrating CMS provider data with demand analytics, you can make informed decisions about market opportunities, competitive landscapes, and service gaps in your target areas. This data is essential for healthcare businesses planning expansion, conducting market research, or optimizing their strategic positioning.
